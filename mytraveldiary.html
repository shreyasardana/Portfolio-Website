Travelling has made me realize that no matter how much I know, there’s always more to learn. Let’s take a look at some beautiful places I’ve been to.

MUSSOORIE

Among the crown jewels of tourism in Uttarakhand, this one is known for vivid ancient temples, waterfalls and the traditional Mall Road.

JAIPUR

India’s first planned city and the largest in Rajasthan. It is also known as The Pink City of India. It evokes the royal family that once ruled the region.

MASHOBRA HILLS

Surrounded by dense coniferous forests, it is one of the most high profile hill stations of Himachal. A place with the most serene ambience.

MANALI

A magical snow-covered place in winter at the northern end of Kullu Valley in Himachal.

SRINAGAR

Northernmost city of India known for its waterfronts, houseboats and traditional Kashmiri handicrafts.

KERELA

Spice Garden of India and the Land of Coconuts, known for its beaches, ayurvedic tourism and its tropical greenery.
